About

Exceed

Exceed is a SAAS (Software as a Service) product designed to help businesses have full automation and control of processes within the organization on a daily basis. This system is designed to synchronize and back up data both locally and over a network to Exceed Cloud, which is powered by Exceed Ecosystem, and is designed to manage slow network connectivity. This platform keeps track of activities in the business such as; recording of business transactions, sales of stock with any and all measurement units, readjust the unit price for old and new stock of the same item to avoid losses in time of market price fluctuation, provision of financial statements/reports to help businesses understand their financial status and growth, receipts for clients to keep track of expenses, provides profit and loss margins, organizes business expenses, provide status of business sustainability and runway, monitors customer acquisition rate for business growth, lifetime customers and other benefits across a wide variety of disciplines.

Furthermore, with the installation of this platform in different businesses, Exceed Cloud which is powered by Exceed Ecosystem, categorizes and synchronizes all data from these different businesses to help business owners keep track of them on a daily basis by monitoring their performances. Exceed performs categorization of datasets and automatically builds a portfolio for banks and corporative unions to grant loans to these business owners. Exceed integrates artificial intelligence to provide expert advice in scaling and maintaining the business, monitors employee activities and expenditures.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

FAQ

What is Exceed?

Exceed is a software designed to help small, medium and big enterprises/businesses have full automation and control of processes within the organization on a daily basis.

Who can use Exceed?

Everyone who owns a business can use Exceed. It is designed to meet the needs of small, medium and large enterprises/businesses.

How can I use Exceed to improve my business?

Exceed categorizes data which can help you monitor daily expenses, evaluate sales and transactions, employee payments and more. Statistics are produced in the form of reports or analytics that makes you understand the sustainability status of your business, how to cut down expenditures, which stocks sales are better to invest in, which seasons make more sales and many more to improve and scale your business.

Must I need a computer to use Exceed for my business?

Exceed is cross platform, which means you can download a version for your computer, as well as a version for your mobile device. More so, the integration of mobile companion app for Exceed helps synchronizes data with your computer for backup and transaction flexibility.

How can I use Exceed for my business?

Exceed is now available for installation on your desktop PC and laptop computer, after installation, you can enjoy the variety of benefits that come with it.
